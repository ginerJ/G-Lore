RACOON: Spawn on Forests, Snowy Taigas and Bamboo Jungles. They are mischievous creatures that will steal edible items from your hands and pollen from bees, after eating the looted nutrients they will become fat and avoid the player, dropping Racoon Fur before deflating. They can be tamed with Melon Slices and bred with Chiken Eggs. A happy racoon will drop Racoon Fur from brushing.
